Why Use Tolza Image Tools?

Tolza provides a complete suite of free online image tools that work entirely in your browser. Unlike other services that require uploading your files to a server, our tools process everything locally on your device — meaning your images stay private and the results are instant.

Complete Image Editing Suite

Whether you need to compress images for your website, resize photos for social media, crop images to specific dimensions, or convert between formats like PNG, JPG, and WebP — Tolza has you covered with 10 specialized tools designed for everyday tasks.

Built for Speed and Privacy

Every tool uses the HTML5 Canvas API for client-side processing. Your images never leave your device, making Tolza one of the most privacy-friendly image tools available. There are no upload limits, no file size restrictions, and no account required.

  • No upload required — files stay on your device
  • Instant results — no waiting for server processing
  • Free forever — no premium plans or hidden fees
  • Works offline — most tools work without internet after loading

Who Is This For?

Tolza image tools are perfect for bloggers, web developers, social media managers, students, and anyone who needs quick image editing without installing software. From compressing images for faster page loads to creating PDFs from photos, these tools save you time every day.
